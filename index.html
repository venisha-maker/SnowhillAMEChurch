About Our Church

Snowhill A.M.E Church is dedicated to serving God, uplifting our community, and spreading the message of hope and love.

We offer worship services, youth programs, outreach ministries, and community support.

Snowhill A.M.E Church was founded in 1906 and has remained a staple of the community.

We welcome all friends and family that are seeking a family oriented place of worship.
